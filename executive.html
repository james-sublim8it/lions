2025-2026 Executive Board

The Udora Leaskdale Lions Club is led by a dedicated executive board elected annually by the membership. Our leaders bring experience, enthusiasm, and commitment to serving our community. Our current officers were installed on September 2, 2025 by Past International Director Lion Tom Gordon.

President

Lion Randy Wilkins

Leading the club with vision and dedication, our President oversees all club activities and represents us in the community and at district events.

1st Vice President

Lion Russ Burton

Supporting the President and ready to step in when needed, our Vice President helps coordinate club activities and member engagement. Also serves as Lion Tamer.

Secretary

Lion Robert Einarson

Maintaining accurate records of meetings, correspondence, and club activities. The Secretary ensures our administrative duties are handled professionally.

Treasurer

Lion David Minns

Managing club finances with transparency and accountability, ensuring our fundraising dollars are used effectively for community service. Also serves as Marketing Chair.

Administrator

Lion Ron Robinson

Handling administrative functions and supporting the executive team in various organizational tasks.

Past President

Lion Tom Graham

Providing continuity and guidance based on previous leadership experience. Also serves as Membership Chair and Tail Twister.

Leadership Development

The Udora Leaskdale Lions Club believes in developing leadership skills among all members. We encourage participation in:

  • District Training: Regular workshops and seminars on Lions leadership
  • Committee Work: Hands-on experience in planning and executing projects
  • Mentorship: Experienced members guiding newer Lions
  • Progressive Roles: Opportunity to advance through various leadership positions
