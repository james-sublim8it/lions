Investing in Our Community's Future

The Udora Leaskdale Lions Club is proud to support local students pursuing post-secondary education through our annual Education Bursary program. We believe that investing in education is one of the most important ways we can serve our community's future.

Program Overview

Each year, our club awards educational bursaries to deserving students from our local area who are pursuing post-secondary education. These awards recognize academic achievement, community involvement, and financial need.

Award Amount

Multiple bursaries are awarded annually, with amounts determined based on available funds and the number of qualified applicants.

Application Period

Applications are typically available in early spring, with a deadline in late spring. Exact dates are announced annually.

Award Presentation

Recipients are honored at their graduation ceremonies and at a Lions Club meeting, with their families invited to attend.

Eligibility Criteria

To be eligible for an Education Bursary, applicants must meet the following requirements:

Academic Requirements

  • Must be graduating from a local high school or equivalent
  • Must have been accepted to a recognized post-secondary institution
  • Must demonstrate satisfactory academic performance
  • Must be pursuing a full-time program of study

Community Connection

  • Must be a resident of the Udora/Leaskdale area
  • Must demonstrate community involvement or volunteer service
  • Preference given to those with connection to Lions Club activities

Financial Consideration

  • Financial need will be considered in the selection process
  • Applicants may be required to provide documentation of financial circumstances

Application Process

Step 1: Obtain Application

Application forms are available from:

  • Local high school guidance counselors
  • Lions Club members
  • Community center
  • By contacting the club directly

Step 2: Complete Application

The application includes:

  • Personal information and contact details
  • Academic transcripts
  • Proof of acceptance to post-secondary institution
  • Essay on educational goals and community involvement
  • Two letters of recommendation
  • Financial information (if applicable)

Step 3: Submit Application

Complete applications must be submitted by the deadline to:

Udora Leaskdale Lions Club
Education Bursary Committee
[Mailing Address]

Or email to: bursary@udoraleaskdalelionsclub.ca

Step 4: Review and Selection

Applications are reviewed by the Education Bursary Committee, with recipients selected based on the criteria outlined above. All applicants will be notified of the results.

Selection Criteria

Applications are evaluated based on the following factors:

Academic Achievement (30%)

Academic performance, course selection, and educational goals

Community Service (30%)

Volunteer work, community involvement, and leadership activities

Personal Essay (25%)

Quality of written communication and clarity of educational and career goals

Financial Need (15%)

Demonstrated financial circumstances and need for assistance

Past Recipients

Over the years, our Education Bursary program has supported students pursuing diverse fields of study including:

  • Medicine and Healthcare
  • Education and Teaching
  • Engineering and Technology
  • Business and Commerce
  • Arts and Social Sciences
  • Skilled Trades and Technical Programs

We're proud to have helped launch the careers of many successful community members who continue to make positive contributions in their chosen fields.

Supporting the Program

The Education Bursary program is funded through our club's fundraising activities, including:

  • Annual Spaghetti Dinners
  • Community events and activities
  • Direct donations from members and supporters
  • Memorial and honorary donations

How You Can Help

If you'd like to support our Education Bursary program, you can:

  • Attend and support our fundraising events
  • Make a direct donation to the bursary fund
  • Volunteer to help with fundraising activities
  • Spread the word about the program to eligible students
