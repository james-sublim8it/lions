Lions Pavilion Project

Building Community Together

The Lions Pavilion Project represents our club's largest community infrastructure investment. This multi-year initiative will provide a permanent gathering space for community events, celebrations, and activities for generations to come.

Project Overview

The pavilion will be constructed in the community park and will serve as a focal point for local gatherings, festivals, and recreational activities. This covered structure will provide shelter and gathering space regardless of weather conditions.

Pavilion Features

  • Large covered gathering area (approximately 30' x 40')
  • Electrical connections for events and lighting
  • Concrete pad foundation for durability
  • Weather-resistant construction materials
  • Picnic table areas and seating
  • Storage space for community equipment
  • Wheelchair accessible design

Community Benefits

  • Venue for community festivals and celebrations
  • Shelter for outdoor events and activities
  • Meeting space for local organizations
  • Enhanced recreational facilities
  • Increased property values in the area
  • Improved quality of life for residents
  • Tourist attraction for the region

Status: Completed

  • Site selection and approval
  • Architectural design development
  • Municipal permits and approvals
  • Environmental impact assessment

Phase 2: Fundraising (2022-2024)

Status: In Progress

  • Community fundraising campaigns
  • Grant applications and awards
  • Corporate sponsorship development
  • Special events and activities

Phase 3: Construction (2024-2025)

Status: Current Phase

  • Site preparation and excavation
  • Foundation pouring and curing
  • Structure assembly and roofing
  • Electrical and finishing work

Phase 4: Completion (2025)

Status: Planned

  • Final inspections and approvals
  • Landscaping and site beautification
  • Official opening ceremony
  • Community celebration event

How You Can Help

The pavilion project depends on community support. There are several ways you can contribute to this important initiative:

Financial Donations

Direct contributions help us reach our funding goal. All donations are tax-deductible and will be acknowledged on our donor recognition board.

  • Platinum Sponsor ($5,000+): Permanent plaque recognition
  • Gold Sponsor ($2,500+): Large donor board listing
  • Silver Sponsor ($1,000+): Donor board recognition
  • Community Supporter ($100+): Thank you listing

In-Kind Donations

We welcome donations of materials, services, or equipment that can reduce project costs:

  • Construction materials and supplies
  • Professional services (engineering, architectural)
  • Equipment rental or usage
  • Skilled labor and expertise

Volunteer Support

Volunteers can help with various aspects of the project:

  • Fundraising events and activities
  • Site preparation and cleanup
  • Construction assistance (as appropriate)
  • Project promotion and awareness

Memorial & Honorary Donations

Honor loved ones with a donation to the pavilion project. Memorial and honorary donations will be specially recognized.

Recognition & Legacy

All supporters of the Lions Pavilion Project will be recognized for their contributions to this lasting community asset.

Donor Recognition

A permanent donor recognition board will be installed at the pavilion site, listing all contributors by category. This ensures that your support will be remembered for generations to come.

Opening Ceremony

All donors and supporters will be invited to the official opening ceremony, where we'll celebrate this community achievement together.

Ongoing Recognition

The pavilion will serve as a lasting tribute to community cooperation and the spirit of service that defines the Lions Club movement.

Project Updates

Stay informed about construction progress and milestones:

September 2024 Update

Foundation work is complete and curing. Electrical conduit installation in progress. Weather permitting, we expect to begin structure assembly in October.

July 2024 Update

Site preparation completed ahead of schedule. Municipal inspections passed. Foundation excavation scheduled for August with concrete pour planned for early September.
